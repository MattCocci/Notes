$\mathbf{x}_i$ is a $k\times1$ vector, and 
$\beta$ is a $k \times 1$ vector of coefficients.
We then represent our model in matrix notation as 
\begin{equation}
    \mathbf{y} = \mathbf{X} \beta + \mathbf{e}
\end{equation}
